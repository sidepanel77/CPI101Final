CURIE

The First AR Quarantine Buddy

We are working on developing an all in one AR companion to increase the feeling of connection that this difficult time is so void of.

Our AR companion, Curie, will be there to lift you up when these trying times are getting you down.

Whether it be a phone, computer, or tablet, Curie will function on any device with a screen and a camera regardless of the OS you happen to be running.

Feelings of loneliness and anxiety are almost universal this year, and we seek to help alleviate those. Curie may just be the answer.

While not the same as a real human, Curie can be called on at every moment and will always have uplifting advice and comments to offer.

Whether you're just lonely or in need of a source of positivity in your day-to-day, Curie is the companion for you.
